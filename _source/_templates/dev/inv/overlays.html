Overlay

Display content in front of the current page. The `overlayIsVisible` global variable is available to the Vue root and it changes based on the `show-overlay` and `hide-overlay` events.

 
<button @click="showOverlay('overlay_1')">Show Overlay</button>
<overlay overlay-id="overlay_1" v-cloak>
  <div class="c_video">
    <video data-lazy-load data-src="https://starter.wbrowar.com/video/clip.mp4" controls></video>
    <noscript><video src="https://starter.wbrowar.com/video/clip.mp4" controls></video></noscript>
  </div>
</overlay>
